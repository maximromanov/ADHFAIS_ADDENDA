

When sport intersects with fascism, communities live under the shadows of negative influences that seek to transform sport from a mere means of entertainment, challenge, and solidarity into a tool of control and ideological manipulation. Sport is one of the most important activities that brings people together from all over the world, regardless of their cultural and social backgrounds. However, when fascism is used for its malicious purposes, sport can lose its positive meanings and become a means to spread hatred and promote divisions.

Historically, athletes have been considered idols within societies; they represent ambition, determination, and the efforts made to achieve success and victory. But sometimes, this idolatry is abused and distorted when fascism takes hold of the sports community. Fascist vision is limited to achieving dominance and superiority, contributing to the spread of violence and hostility among enthusiastic crowds.

The presence of fascism in sport is evident through several aspects. For example, game rules may deliberately be altered in favor of one team or to restrict freedoms and popular rights. In addition, fascist governments may interfere in sports associations and organizations to promote their political agendas.

Fascism also uses sport as a means of propaganda and encouraging extremism. Fascist governments organize massive sports tournaments aimed at tarnishing the image of adversaries and creating a positive image for the fascist regime. Sport is used to distract people from real problems within society and redirect their focus towards sporting victories.

Furthermore, fascist regimes attempt to control players and teams through strict discipline and rigid rules. Political symbols and expressions opposing the regime are prohibited, and complete loyalty and allegiance to fascism are imposed. Penalties are imposed on athletes who criticize the regime, ensuring everyone obeys the fascist agenda.

In the end, we must be cautious and vigilant towards the influence of fascism in sport. This requires education and awareness, as well as solidarity with players and crowds who face persecution due to their political opinions and beliefs. We must all strive to uphold the values of positive sport and promote it as a tool for engagement, understanding, and overcoming divisions and discrimination.

تاريخياً، اُعتُبِر الرياضيون أيقونات داخل المجتمعات؛ يمثلون الطموح والعزيمة والجهود المبذولة لتحقيق النجاح والانتصار. ولكن في بعض الأحيان، يتم استغلال وتشويه هذا العبادة عندما تسيطر الفاشية على مجتمع الرياضة. رؤية الفاشية محدودة لتحقيق الهيمنة والتفوق، مسهمة في انتشار العنف والعداء بين الجماهير المتحمسة.

علاوة على ذلك، تحاول الأنظمة الفاشية السيطرة على اللاعبين والفرق من خلال الانضباط الصارم والقواعد الصلبة. تُحظر الرموز السياسية والتعبيرات المعارضة للنظام، ويُفرض الولاء الكامل والانتماء للفاشية. تُفرض عقوبات على الرياضيين الذين ينتقدون النظام، لضمان أن الجميع يطيع أجندة الفاشية.
