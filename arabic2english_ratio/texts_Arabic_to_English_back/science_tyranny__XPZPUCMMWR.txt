

In recent times, science and technology have witnessed tremendous progress in various fields, whether in medicine, engineering, or even space sciences. These advancements are considered a great blessing for humanity as they work towards improving the quality of life and increasing opportunities for development and prosperity.

However, the directions and considerations of science can be taken advantage of by oppressive governing systems, making them tools of injustice and domination. Scientific tyranny is a term that may seem unfamiliar at first, but it refers to the misuse of scientific and technological tools by non-democratic or dictatorial governments to impose their control and eliminate individual freedom and rights.

Repressive governments are attractive for the use of science and technology for several reasons. They work to obscure the truth by falsifying or concealing scientific evidence, exposing the reality of things to deviation and deception among members of society. They also try to distort science to serve their personal purposes, for example, through the use of technology to spy on and restrict individuals, or to inform them of what is suitable for them.
