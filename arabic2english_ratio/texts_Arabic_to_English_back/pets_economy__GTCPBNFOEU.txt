

In recent years, the pet industry has witnessed significant growth in the Arab world. Pets have become an integral part of people's lives, and they have many financial demands and requirements necessary for the proper upbringing of their pets. The pet industry is considered one of the emerging businesses that generate lucrative profits.

There are many factors that affect the cost of pets, ranging from the type and breed of the animal to the necessary supplies and food they consume. For example, dogs and cats are considered the most popular pets in the Arab world, and owning and caring for these animals incurs continuous financial costs.

The costs of owning pets include daily care for food, healthcare, and essential supplies such as beds, water bowls, and toys, in addition to necessary vaccinations and regular veterinary visits. Raising a pet can cost thousands of dollars annually, and the costs vary depending on the type and size of the animal. For instance, the costs of training and distinguishing pedigree dogs are high and include the cost of a trainer and the necessary tools.

However, the technological revolution has provided a range of financial solutions for pet owners. Thanks to smartphone applications, pet owners can access services such as online shopping for pet food and supplies, as well as booking veterinary appointments online.

The role of pets is not limited to financial costs; it also reflects changes in the Arab economy. The continuous increase in demand for pets has led to the prosperity of the animal feed industry and specialized manufacturing companies for pet products. This means that new job opportunities have emerged in this sector, and there are many startups that focus on manufacturing pet products.

In addition, pets have become an important source of income in many households. By breeding and selling pets or providing pet care services, individuals can increase their income and achieve financial independence. Raising pets can be an opportunity to build a successful and sustainable business.

In conclusion, the pet industry in the Arab world reflects the continuous increase in attention to the health and well-being of pets. Pets are not only a luxury for millions of people but have also become an opportunity to bring about economic and social change in the Arab world.
