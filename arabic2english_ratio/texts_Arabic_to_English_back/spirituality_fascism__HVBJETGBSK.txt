

Human history has witnessed a continuous conflict between spirituality and fascism, where oppressive forces have tried to exploit and control faith and spirituality for their personal purposes and to achieve their oppressive and racist agendas. These two forces have clashed in wars characterized by racial extermination and religious bigotry, but they have also deteriorated through a series of acts of violence and murder.

When fascism and spirituality converge, a serious distortion occurs in collective consciousness. Spirituality calls for peace, tolerance, and acceptance, while fascism promotes tyranny, division, and hatred. Spirituality calls for communication with higher powers and learning love and healing, while fascism advocates for extremist racist ideas and military control.

True spirituality cannot be part of any kind of fascism. Fascism compels individuals to blindly obey the leader and seeks to establish domination and repression at the expense of individual freedom, while spirituality urges individuals to connect with their inner selves and helps them achieve inner harmony and spiritual peace.

However, there have been rare cases where spirituality and limited fascist ideologies have come together. Some extremist groups may exploit faith and spirituality to attract believing individuals and promote their extremist causes, using the rigid concepts of the existing elite and controlling individuals. Nevertheless, we must be cautious and understand that these groups distort true spirituality and misuse faith for their personal goals.

Therefore, we must strongly resist any inclination that combines spirituality and fascism, demand freedom of belief and expression, and maintain the values of tolerance and peaceful coexistence among cultures and religions. We must work together to promote cultural diversity and acceptance of different ideas and express our rejection of any attempt to violate human rights as the foundation of our struggle.

In the end, spirituality will remain a call for peace, love, and tolerance. Despite repeated attempts by fascism to exploit it, it will not succeed in robbing humanity from people's hearts. Only noble ideas and true spiritual values will prevail, and we will continue to fight for our rights and freedoms, firmly believing that love will ultimately triumph over hatred and bigotry.
